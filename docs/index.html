Why Using Caveau?

Privacy Database

Caveau is the database for special data that provides all the necessary tools to handle sensitive information and simplify your compliance needs.

Streamline compliance

Generate up-to-date pre-filled compliance audit documents 10x faster.
Automate responses to “User Data Deletion” and “Data Subject Access" requests.

Zero Trust

Centralized policy definition and enforcement coupled with audit logs ensure every resource access is explicitly evaluated.

Continuous Monitoring

Audit the compliance posture in realtime.
Receive alerts anytime a requirement of one of the regulations in the countries you operate in is no longer met.

Compliance by design

Separate sensitive information from the rest of the data before it reaches your database.
Forget compliance and cybersecurity concerns from every downstream process.

Make it your own

Integrate with your authentication system, use an external Key Management System, prefer a Post Quantum Encryption algorithm.
You can configure to better suits your need

Ecosystem Agnostic

Designed to be integrated as part of any infrastructure, you can use it as a SaaS service, deploy it on-premise or in your cloud ecosystem.

Developer friendly

An easy-to-use REST API built by developers for developers: finally Compliance, IT and Development can share the same agenda.

Open source

The core of Caveau is Open Source so everyone can contribute to it adapting to their ecosystem and peer review its security.

Vertical Markets

Fintech

Store the PII and PCI data of your users into Caveau to limit the complexity of your infrastructure and the scope of PCI-DSS compliance, accelerate your time to market avoiding to tackle lots of security and design issues. Integrate with third party services, keeping your environment free of PCI and PII data.

Healthcare

Fast-track your compliance and time to market, focusing on your innovation. Keep your sensible data confidential while remaining usable for analytics, research and sharing with third parties.

Government

Enable complex multi-party interactions, interfacing multiple authentication systems, enforcing access policies and orchestrating data governance.
Avoid costly data duplication, unmanageable data sprawl, mitigate privacy and security risks by reducing the attack surface.

Business

Almost every business operation is impacted by the all encompassing data protection regulations existing today or coming into force.
Anticipating future privacy needs and implementing correct privacy practices is a real opportunity that can differentiate the value offer and open up new opportunities.

Security & Compliance

Reduce risks of data breach by mitigating the impact of SQL injection attacks, compromised servers, file leaks or stolen credentials. Make sure all the data circulating on your infrastructure is free from biometrics, personal, financial and health data.

Machine Learning & AI

Advanced Machine Learning systems need to balance data access against the security implications of that access. Often the owner of data and the developer of machine learning algorithms are different stakeholders. Caveau provides the perfect sandbox where data and algorithms can interact, meeting the security and confidential requirements both need.
